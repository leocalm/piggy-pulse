# ACCOUNT_SCOPE_BEHAVIOR

## Scope Modes
- Single account
- All accounts

## Effects

### Filtering
Single:
- Show transactions impacting that account.
All:
- Show all transactions.

### Quick Add (Desktop)
Single:
- From auto-filled for transfers.
All:
- From selectable.

### Drawer (Mobile)
Same rules as desktop.

## Principle
Scope affects defaults, not data integrity.
